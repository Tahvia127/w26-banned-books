## Data Sources

### PEN America – Banned Books Dataset
Source: PEN America  
Description: Records of book challenges and bans across U.S. states and school districts.  
Geographic Level: State, district  
Years: Various

### U.S. Census Bureau – ACS 5-Year Estimates
Source: data.census.gov  
Tables: DP05 (Demographic and Housing Estimates)  
Geographic Level: State  
Years: 2023
Description: Population and demographic characteristics used for state-level analysis.

### U.S. Census Bureau – TIGER/Line Shapefiles
Source: U.S. Census Bureau  
File: tl_2025_us_state
Geographic Level: State  
Description: State boundary shapefile used for choropleth mapping and geographic visualization.

### Election Results Data
Source: MIT Election Data and Science Lab  
Geographic Level: State  
Years: 2020 (2024 pending availability)  
Description: State-level presidential election results used to classify political alignment.

Source: Bucknell Digital Commons
Geographic Level: County/State  
Years: 2024
Description: State and county level presidential election results to supplement (potentially) MIT election data. 2020/2024 election results are included.
